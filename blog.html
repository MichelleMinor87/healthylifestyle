Lower your cholesterol with these foods!

Benefits of Fruits

Carbohydrates, fiber, vitamin C, and potassium are all present in most fruits. Having fruit in your diet provides you with vitamins, antioxidants, and dietary fiber that are beneficial for your body. Various fruits can improve a person's health and lower their risk of inflammation...

Benefits of Grains

Grains can give you protein, minerals, fiber, and plant compounds. They can also lower your risk of heart disease, reduce the risk of obesity, and lower the risk of type 2 diabetes and stroke. They help with digestion and help with chronic inflammation...

Benefits of Legumes

Among legumes are peanuts, kidney, black-eyed, lima and other beans. These beans contain fats, iron, and protein. It reduces cholesterol and blood pressure by increasing the level of antioxidants. In addition, it helps control weight. It contains compounds known as antinutrients as well.
